Black Hole

A digital version of the classic Black Hole strategy board game, featuring local multiplayer and AI opponents.

SwiftUI iOS Game Development

Overview

Black Hole brings the classic abstract strategy board game to iOS. Players take turns placing numbered tiles on the board, carefully planning their moves while anticipating their opponent's strategy.

When the board is almost full, one empty space remains—the Black Hole. The surrounding tile values determine the winner, rewarding careful planning over luck.

Gameplay

  • Play against another player on the same device.
  • Challenge an AI opponent.
  • Simple and intuitive touch controls.
  • Clean board-game focused interface.
  • Fast games suitable for casual play.

Why I Built It

This project allowed me to explore game development on iOS while implementing the rules and strategy of a well-known abstract board game. The focus was on creating a smooth user experience, responsive gameplay, and an AI opponent that provides an enjoyable challenge.
